Rethink everything you know about sales

People hate being sold to‚Ä¶
but we love to buy.

If you understand this dynamic, you can sell anything. As a freelancer, you will always have something to sell: your skills and style!

It‚Äôs not that they didn‚Äôt want to respond, it‚Äôs that they were up to their eyeballs in other projects and some things took a back seat. By now you hopefully understand the importance of following up. It‚Äôs about getting the attention of the person you‚Äôre targeting, sometimes which will take months. Remember, the downside of not following up is:

  1. They forget about you
  2. Someone else earns their attention

The way you think about when you send your follow-up emails and how much time in between each one is what we call cadence. Rebecca Matias dives into sales cadence examples and lessons:
